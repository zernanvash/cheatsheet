# cryptpad — crackmes.one RE CTF 2026

**Origin:** crackmes.one  
**Category:** Reverse Engineering  
**Platform:** Linux  

## Challenge Description

A cryptography-focused crackme challenge from the crackmes.one RE CTF 2026. Participants must analyze the binary and recover the hidden flag through reverse engineering.

## Challenge Files

Challenge binary/handout files are available in the `handout/` directory.

## Solution

Analyze the binary with a disassembler (e.g., Ghidra, IDA Pro, Binary Ninja) to understand the encryption logic, then reverse the algorithm to extract or derive the flag.
